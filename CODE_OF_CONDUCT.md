# Code of Conduct

Market Pulse AI is an open-source project for financial research tooling. We want the community to be respectful, constructive, and safe for contributors from different backgrounds.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent while asking for clarification when needed.
- Give feedback that is specific and actionable.
- Respect different levels of experience.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and user safety.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Spamming, trolling, or repeated off-topic disruption.
- Pushing misleading investment claims or guaranteed return language.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.
