# Vision

Market Pulse AI exists to make financial research more understandable, transparent, and risk-aware for Thai and international users.

## Principles

- Education first, not financial advice.
- Transparent data sources and cautious interpretation.
- Bilingual access for Thai and English users.
- Open-source governance and community review.
- Modular architecture for web, mobile, desktop, and future API providers.

## Product Direction

Market Pulse AI should grow into a professional investment research terminal with:

- Real market data normalization.
- AI investment committee perspectives.
- Asset comparison and portfolio research.
- Economic calendar and news impact analysis.
- Safe AI Q&A that explains uncertainty and risk.
