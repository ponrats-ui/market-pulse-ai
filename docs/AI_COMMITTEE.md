# AI Investment Committee

Sprint 2 replaces the single analysis surface with a multi-specialist investment committee layout.

## Chief Investment AI

The summary card appears near the top of the dashboard and includes:

- Current view
- Confidence
- Risk score
- Volatility
- Main opportunity
- Main risk
- Balanced AI summary

The summary avoids deterministic investment language and keeps a risk-focused tone.

## Specialist Panels

Five specialist panels are shown:

1. Technical Analyst: trend, RSI, MACD, EMA, volume, chart structure
2. Fundamental Analyst: revenue, profit, cash flow, debt, ROE, ROA, valuation
3. Macro Economist: interest rates, inflation, DXY, oil, gold, bond yields
4. News Analyst: sentiment, headline risk, industry events, earnings events
5. Risk Officer: portfolio risk, volatility, drawdown, concentration, liquidity

## Safety Policy

The committee must not present outcomes as fixed. It should explain uncertainty, identify invalidation conditions, and preserve the financial advice disclaimer.
