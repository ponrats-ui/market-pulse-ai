## Purpose

AI ethics defines how Market Pulse AI should behave when using AI to support financial research.

## Transparency

AI outputs should disclose evidence, missing data, confidence, limitations, and uncertainty.

## Fairness

The product should avoid unfairly favoring assets, markets, providers, or viewpoints without evidence.

## Bias Reduction

Market Pulse AI should reduce bias by using structured evidence, multiple perspectives, and clear disclosure of uncertainty.

## Explainability

AI should explain the reasoning behind outputs in language that users can understand.

## No Manipulation

AI should not manipulate users into action. It should not use urgency, fear, greed, or false certainty to influence decisions.

## No Emotional Persuasion

Financial AI should remain calm and disciplined. It should avoid emotionally charged language that pressures users.

## Evidence-Based Output

AI output should be based on available evidence. Missing evidence should be stated, not invented.

## Responsible AI

Responsible AI means:

- Clear limitations
- Cautious recommendations
- No guaranteed language
- No fabricated data
- No hidden confidence inflation
- Respect for user judgment

## User Autonomy

The user should be able to understand, question, ignore, or override the AI view.

## Human Decision Authority

The human remains the decision maker.

Market Pulse AI assists. Humans decide.
