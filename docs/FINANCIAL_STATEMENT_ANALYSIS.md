# Financial Statement Analysis

Financial statement analysis is mainly applicable to stocks. It should not be shown as a stock-style statement review for crypto, commodities, indices, FX, or macro assets.

## Stock Metrics

- revenue trend
- net profit trend
- gross margin
- net margin
- debt to equity
- cash flow quality
- ROE
- ROA
- EPS
- P/E
- P/BV
- dividend yield
- 3-5 year trend placeholder
- balance sheet strength
- earnings quality
- valuation risk

## Non-Stock Assets

Show this message:

`Financial statement analysis is not applicable to this asset type`

Then provide alternative relevant fundamentals such as liquidity, supply/demand, network activity, macro sensitivity, policy context, or constituent earnings.

## Safety

The analysis must remain cautious and avoid single-metric conclusions. It should explain uncertainty and remind users that it is not financial advice.
