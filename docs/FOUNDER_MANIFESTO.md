## Why Market Pulse AI Exists

Market Pulse AI exists because investors are surrounded by information but often lack clear, trustworthy interpretation.

Markets produce endless prices, charts, headlines, opinions, and noise. The difficult work is not only seeing data. The difficult work is understanding what matters, why it matters, what is missing, and what risks could change the view.

Market Pulse AI exists to help users think more clearly.

## The Long-Term Mission

The long-term mission is to build a Global AI Investment Intelligence Platform.

The platform should not merely analyze markets after opportunities are obvious. It should help investors discover high-quality opportunities earlier through transparent evidence, cross-market context, disciplined risk analysis, and explainable AI.

The goal is not to predict the future. The goal is to improve the quality of research and decision-making.

## The Responsibility of Explainable AI

Financial AI has a responsibility to be humble.

Markets are uncertain. Data can be incomplete. Providers can fail. Narratives can mislead. A responsible AI system must disclose uncertainty instead of hiding it.

Market Pulse AI should explain:

- What evidence was used
- What evidence was missing
- Why a view exists
- Which risks matter
- What could invalidate the view
- How confident the system is

AI should be a research partner, not an authority demanding obedience.

## Evidence Over Hype

Market Pulse AI is committed to evidence, not hype.

The product must never recommend based only on price, momentum, or market excitement. Every opportunity should be supported by measurable evidence and clear risk context.

If the evidence is weak, the product should say so.

If data is missing, the product should say so.

If confidence is low, the product should say so.

## Opportunity Discovery

Market Pulse AI does not search for cheap stocks. It searches for asymmetric opportunities.

An opportunity can come from quality, growth, value, momentum, catalysts, market structure, cross-market relationships, or under-recognized change. Price is only one signal.

Penny stock is a category, not an investment thesis.

Multi-bagger similarity is a research framework, not a prediction.

Catalysts are useful only when supported by evidence.

Risk penalties protect users from confusing excitement with quality.

## User Trust

Trust is the product.

Every feature, score, chart, panel, and AI response should protect user trust by being honest, explainable, and disciplined.

The product should help users become better investors, not more impulsive ones.

## Permanent Commitments

Market Pulse AI commits to:

- Real data where available
- Transparent unavailable states
- Zero Mock Policy
- Explainable AI
- Evidence-based opportunity discovery
- Conservative financial language
- Human decision authority
- Documentation as part of Definition of Done
- Production verification before approval
- Long-term maintainability
