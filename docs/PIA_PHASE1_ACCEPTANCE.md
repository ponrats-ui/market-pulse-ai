# PIA Phase 1 Acceptance

## Branch

`feature/pia-founder-experience`

## Mission

Founder Experience Refinement focuses on making Market Pulse AI feel complete, obvious, and ready for a daily investor workflow. This sprint does not deploy, merge, push, or tag.

## Product Identity

- Header now presents `Market Pulse AI` with `Personal Investment Assistant (PIA)`.
- Header and footer include `Powered by PIA™`.
- Footer includes `© 2026 Market Pulse AI`, `Powered by PIA™`, and `All Rights Reserved.`

## Experience Refinements

- Asset Center keeps category above search and supports live autocomplete from existing search.
- Watchlist keeps the current UX while adding favorite language, sorting, loading copy, context actions, and reorder-ready controls.
- Technical chart supports `1D`, `5D`, `1M`, `3M`, `6M`, `YTD`, `1Y`, `5Y`, and `MAX`, plus visible indicators, crosshair tooltip, and brush zoom.
- AI Committee now shows evidence, confidence, vote, pros, cons, and reasoning for each member, with a Chief AI summary.
- Fundamental Analysis shows Thai explanation and transparent unavailable states.
- Risk Engine shows severity, probability, evidence, mitigation, historical context, and risk trend.
- Compare adds radar chart, correlation matrix, relative strength, and thesis summary.
- AI Assistant starts from the selected asset, includes suggested prompts, conversation history, follow-ups, and referenced facts.
- Paper Trading shows market value, cash, daily return, total return, allocation pie, sector allocation, realized P/L, risk, diversification, and transaction count.
- PIA Relax Mode remains optional, never autoplays, uses configurable YouTube embed sources, and does not block market data.

## Data Integrity

No fake prices, fake news, fabricated calendar events, or fabricated AI certainty were added. Missing provider data remains unavailable.

## Decision

READY FOR FOUNDER WALKTHROUGH.
