# PIA Recommendation Policy

## Allowed Educational Actions

- Accumulate
- Buy
- Hold
- Wait
- Reduce
- Avoid
- Sell

## Rules

- Recommendations are educational research outputs, not trading instructions.
- No guaranteed language is allowed.
- Low-confidence outputs default toward `Wait`.
- Risk and missing data can override constructive technical or fundamental signals.
- Human users decide whether to act.
