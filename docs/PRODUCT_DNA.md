## Purpose

Product DNA defines the permanent beliefs behind Market Pulse AI.

Future developers, AI assistants, contributors, and reviewers should use this document to understand why the product exists, not only how it works.

Market Pulse AI is a Global AI Investment Intelligence Platform focused on explainable opportunity discovery.

## Core Beliefs

- Investors need better reasoning, not louder signals.
- AI should explain before it recommends.
- Real market data is more valuable than artificial completeness.
- Missing data must stay visible.
- Opportunity discovery requires evidence, risk context, and confidence disclosure.
- Price is only one signal.
- A cheap stock is not automatically an opportunity.
- User trust is the product.

## Core Values

- Explainability
- Evidence
- Transparency
- Caution
- User control
- Global perspective
- Cross-market thinking
- Production discipline
- Documentation as product memory
- Long-term maintainability

## Investment Philosophy

Market Pulse AI does not search for cheap stocks. It searches for asymmetric opportunities.

An asymmetric opportunity is a research candidate where potential reward, evidence quality, catalyst context, market structure, and risk discipline may justify deeper investigation.

The product should help users understand:

- Why an asset appears
- Which evidence supports it
- Which risks weaken it
- What is missing
- What could change the view
- Whether confidence is high, medium, or low

The user remains the final decision maker.

## AI Philosophy

AI in Market Pulse AI should be:

- Explainable
- Evidence-based
- Conservative
- Honest about uncertainty
- Clear about missing data
- Separated from hype
- Structured for learning

AI must never fabricate prices, charts, news, financial values, catalysts, recommendations, or confidence.

## Engineering Philosophy

Engineering decisions should protect trust.

The system should be:

- Simple where possible
- Precise where necessary
- Observable in production
- Defensive around provider failure
- Clear about unavailable data
- Documentation-aligned
- Testable
- Maintainable

One prompt should produce one logical change. Unrelated changes should not be mixed into a commit.

## Founder Philosophy

The founder vision is to build a trusted AI investment intelligence platform that helps investors discover opportunities earlier and understand them better.

The product should not encourage blind action. It should improve user judgment.

Every major product decision should ask whether the change increases intelligence, explainability, and trust.

## Design Principle

Every future feature must answer three questions:

1. Does it improve investment intelligence?
2. Can AI explain why?
3. Does it increase user trust?

If the answer is no, the feature should not exist.

## Long-Term Vision

Market Pulse AI should become a global platform that discovers high-quality investment opportunities before they become widely recognized.

The objective is not prediction. The objective is intelligent discovery through transparent evidence, cross-market context, disciplined risk analysis, and explainable AI.
