## Purpose

This document defines how Market Pulse AI should be positioned for users, contributors, founders, investors, and future enterprise customers.

## What Market Pulse AI Is

Market Pulse AI is an Explainable AI Investment Intelligence Platform.

It helps investors understand markets through real data, transparent scoring, cautious AI-assisted analysis, cross-market context, and clear unavailable states when evidence is missing.

The product exists to improve decision quality, not to replace investor judgment.

## What Market Pulse AI Is Not

Market Pulse AI is not:

- A trading bot
- A guaranteed signal generator
- A black-box recommendation engine
- A promise of profit
- A broker or execution platform
- A replacement for professional financial advice

## Target Users

Primary users:

- Self-directed investors
- Thai and global market participants
- Long-term investors
- Beginner investors who need education and context
- Experienced investors who want explainable market scanning

Future users:

- Professional analysts
- Investment educators
- Enterprise research teams
- Financial platforms requiring explainable intelligence layers

## Core Value Proposition

Market Pulse AI helps users answer:

- What is happening in the market?
- Why might it matter?
- What evidence supports the view?
- What data is missing?
- What risks should be monitored?
- What should the user research next?

The value is clarity, context, trust, and time saved.

## Product Differentiation

Market Pulse AI differentiates through:

- Explainable AI rather than unexplained signals
- Zero Mock Policy rather than artificial completeness
- Multi-factor scoring rather than one-dimensional indicators
- Cross-market thinking rather than isolated asset views
- Thai-first and global-ready user experience
- Professional dashboard design with educational transparency

## Competitive Context

### Bloomberg

Bloomberg is an institutional-grade market terminal with deep data and workflows. Market Pulse AI does not compete on data breadth today. It competes on accessible explainability, transparent AI, and a lower-friction research experience.

### TradingView

TradingView is strong in charts, community, and technical workflows. Market Pulse AI should complement charting with explainable AI context, risk interpretation, and market-adjusted analysis.

### Yahoo Finance

Yahoo Finance is a broad market information portal. Market Pulse AI should turn market information into structured reasoning and explainable insight.

### Google Finance

Google Finance is simple and accessible. Market Pulse AI should preserve simplicity while adding deeper educational and AI-assisted interpretation.

### Seeking Alpha

Seeking Alpha has strong commentary and contributor analysis. Market Pulse AI should focus on transparent, evidence-based AI summaries and multi-factor structure.

### Simply Wall St

Simply Wall St emphasizes visual fundamental analysis. Market Pulse AI should combine visual clarity with broader market context and explainable AI views.

### Morningstar

Morningstar is respected for research and fund analysis. Market Pulse AI should differentiate through real-time explainable intelligence and cross-market context.

## Explainable AI Advantage

Explainable AI is the competitive advantage because investors do not only need answers. They need reasons.

A trusted investment assistant must show:

- Evidence used
- Missing evidence
- Risk factors
- Confidence
- Conditions that could change the view
- Limits of the analysis

Explainability turns AI from a black box into a research partner.

## Transparency Over Prediction

Prediction can create false confidence. Transparency creates trust.

Market Pulse AI should not attempt to sound certain about uncertain markets. The platform should help users understand probabilities, trade-offs, data quality, risk, and context.

Transparency is more durable than prediction because it remains valuable even when markets change.
