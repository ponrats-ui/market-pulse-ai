## Mission

Market Pulse AI is an Explainable AI investment assistant.

The goal is not to predict the future. The goal is to help investors make better informed decisions using transparent analysis, real market data when available, and clear disclosure when data is missing or uncertain.

Market Pulse AI should help users understand what is happening, why it may matter, what evidence is available, what remains unknown, and which risks deserve attention before they make their own decision.

## Core Principles

- **Explainable AI:** AI outputs must show the reasoning path behind conclusions.
- **Zero Mock Policy:** Production must never present fabricated prices, charts, news, financial values, or recommendations as real.
- **Transparency:** Missing, delayed, partial, stale, or unavailable data must be stated clearly.
- **Multi-factor analysis:** Market views should combine price action, fundamentals, macro context, news, risk, and portfolio exposure where data exists.
- **Human-centered UX:** The product should reduce cognitive load and help users act with discipline, not pressure.
- **Deterministic scoring where appropriate:** Scores should be reproducible when based on the same inputs and configuration.
- **Evidence-based recommendations:** Recommendations must be educational, cautious, and supported by visible evidence.
- **Context-aware analysis:** Asset analysis should consider relevant market relationships instead of treating every asset in isolation.

## Product Philosophy

AI should explain, not merely recommend.

Every score should have a reason. Every recommendation should show supporting evidence, limitations, and conditions that could change the view. Missing data should never become fabricated data.

Market Pulse AI must remain a decision-support system, not a decision-maker. The user remains the final decision maker.

The product should prefer honest uncertainty over false precision. It should avoid black-box conclusions where an explainable structure can provide safer guidance.

## Target Users

Market Pulse AI is designed for:

- Self-directed investors who want clearer market context.
- Thai and global market participants who need bilingual financial research support.
- Beginners who need plain-language education before advanced tools.
- Experienced investors who want fast market scanning, explainable signals, and portfolio context.
- Founders, maintainers, and contributors who need a stable product standard for future work.

## Long-Term Product Direction

The long-term direction is to become a professional personal investment intelligence workspace that can:

- Monitor global assets across markets and asset classes.
- Explain cross-market relationships.
- Combine asset-level signals with market-level context.
- Track portfolio exposure, concentration, and risk.
- Provide transparent AI committee viewpoints.
- Deliver useful alerts and summaries without overwhelming the user.
- Preserve trust through clear evidence, disclaimers, and unavailable states.

## Founder Standard

Future development should protect the trust model of the product:

- Keep explainability above complexity.
- Keep production behavior above demo behavior.
- Keep user control above automation.
- Keep evidence above opinion.
- Keep transparent unavailable states above fake completeness.
