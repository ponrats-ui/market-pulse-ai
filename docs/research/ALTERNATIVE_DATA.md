# Alternative Data

## Purpose

Alternative data refers to non-traditional information that may help investors understand business activity, market behavior, or economic change.

Market Pulse AI may eventually use alternative data where legal, ethical, licensed, and explainable.

## Possible Data Categories

- Web traffic
- App rankings
- Search trends
- Supply chain indicators
- Shipping data
- Satellite observations
- Job postings
- Product reviews
- Social attention
- Developer activity
- Public filings metadata

## Product Relevance

Alternative data may support early opportunity discovery by identifying change before it appears fully in financial statements.

It should never replace fundamentals, risk analysis, or provider attribution.

## Governance Requirements

Any future alternative data must be:

- Legally usable
- Ethically acceptable
- Properly licensed or public
- Attributed where required
- Explainable
- Validated
- Clearly limited

## Limitations

Alternative data can be noisy, biased, incomplete, or misleading.

Market Pulse AI should treat it as supporting evidence, not final proof.
