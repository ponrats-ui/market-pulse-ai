# Behavioral Finance

## Purpose

Behavioral finance studies how human psychology affects investment decisions and market behavior.

Market Pulse AI uses behavioral concepts to help users avoid impulsive decisions and understand market mood.

## Common Biases

- Overconfidence
- Recency bias
- Herd behavior
- Loss aversion
- Confirmation bias
- Anchoring
- FOMO
- Panic selling

## Product Relevance

The product should help users slow down and ask better questions.

AI explanations, risk warnings, confidence levels, and unavailable states should reduce emotional decision-making.

## Limitations

Behavioral signals are difficult to measure precisely. The product should avoid overclaiming psychological interpretations and should focus on observable evidence.
