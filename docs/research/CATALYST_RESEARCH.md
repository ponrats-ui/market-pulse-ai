# Catalyst Research

## Purpose

Catalyst research studies events or changes that may alter investor expectations.

Catalysts can explain why an opportunity may become recognized by the market.

## Catalyst Types

- New products
- Government policy
- Contracts
- Mergers and acquisitions
- Expansion
- Technology shifts
- Industry cycles
- Supply chain changes
- AI
- Semiconductors
- Defense
- Energy
- Infrastructure
- Healthcare
- Biotech

## Product Relevance

Market Pulse AI should identify catalysts only when evidence exists.

The product should distinguish confirmed catalysts from potential catalysts and unsupported narratives.

## Limitations

Catalysts can fail, be delayed, already be priced in, or have smaller-than-expected effects.

The product should explain catalyst uncertainty.
