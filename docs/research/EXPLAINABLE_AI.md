# Explainable AI Research

## Purpose

Explainable AI focuses on making AI outputs understandable, reviewable, and contestable by humans.

For Market Pulse AI, explainability is not optional. It is the foundation of user trust.

## Core Concepts

- Evidence visibility
- Clear reasoning
- Uncertainty disclosure
- Missing-data disclosure
- Confidence explanation
- Human decision authority
- Auditability
- Model limitations

## Product Relevance

Every recommendation, opportunity score, risk warning, and AI view should explain why it exists.

The product should not expose proprietary implementation details, but it must expose enough reasoning for users to understand the conclusion.

## Limitations

Explainability does not eliminate uncertainty. A well-explained view can still be wrong because markets change.

The product should explain uncertainty, not pretend to remove it.
