# Market Microstructure

## Purpose

Market microstructure studies how trading, liquidity, order flow, spreads, and market mechanics affect prices.

It is especially important for small-cap, micro-cap, and penny-stock research.

## Core Concepts

- Liquidity
- Bid-ask spread
- Volume
- Market depth
- Float
- Slippage
- Trading halts
- Price discovery
- Volatility from thin trading

## Product Relevance

Market Pulse AI should penalize opportunities when liquidity is poor, spreads are wide, or trading conditions make the asset difficult to enter or exit.

Thinly traded assets can show dramatic price movement without meaningful institutional confirmation.

## Limitations

Microstructure data may be limited or delayed depending on provider access.

When data is unavailable, the product should disclose the limitation rather than assume liquidity quality.
