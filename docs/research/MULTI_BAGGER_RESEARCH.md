# Multi-Bagger Research

## Purpose

Multi-bagger research studies companies that delivered exceptional long-term returns.

Market Pulse AI uses this research to identify measurable traits that may deserve attention in current companies. It does not predict future winners.

## Common Measurable Traits

- Revenue acceleration
- Margin expansion
- Large industry tailwind
- Market creation
- Capital efficiency
- Founder or insider alignment
- Institutional accumulation
- Product innovation
- Execution quality
- Competitive moat
- Expanding addressable market
- Improving unit economics

## Historical Pattern Awareness

Historical high-growth companies often combined multiple reinforcing forces: improving fundamentals, expanding markets, strong execution, and changing investor perception.

No single trait is enough.

## Product Relevance

The Multi-Bagger Similarity framework should explain which traits are present, which are absent, and which are unknown.

Similarity should produce research questions, not predictions.

## Limitations

Survivorship bias is serious. Many companies share early traits with winners but fail.

The product must never imply that similarity guarantees future performance.
