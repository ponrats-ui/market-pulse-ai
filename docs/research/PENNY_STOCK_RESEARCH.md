# Penny Stock Research

## Purpose

Penny stock research studies very low-priced or early-stage securities.

Penny stock is a category, not an investment thesis.

## Major Risks

- Pump-and-dump behavior
- Liquidity risk
- Low float
- Dilution
- Reverse splits
- Accounting issues
- Extreme volatility
- Delisting risk
- Wide bid-ask spreads
- Weak disclosure
- Negative cash flow

## Why Some Companies Graduate

Some successful companies may begin with small market capitalization, low awareness, or limited liquidity and later graduate into larger, higher-quality businesses.

Graduation usually requires measurable improvement:

- Revenue growth
- Better margins
- Stronger balance sheet
- Improved liquidity
- Better governance
- Real catalysts
- Institutional recognition

## Product Relevance

Market Pulse AI should never promote penny stocks because they are cheap.

The product should identify whether evidence supports asymmetric opportunity and whether risk penalty is too severe.

## Limitations

Penny stocks can be highly speculative. The product must use conservative language and disclose missing data clearly.
