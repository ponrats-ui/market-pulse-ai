# Quality Investing

## Purpose

Quality investing focuses on durable businesses with strong fundamentals, resilient economics, and disciplined capital allocation.

Quality matters because strong businesses may survive uncertainty better and compound value over time.

## Common Quality Traits

- Consistent revenue
- Strong margins
- Positive free cash flow
- Healthy balance sheet
- High return on equity or assets
- Low financial distress
- Durable competitive advantage
- Good execution
- Rational capital allocation

## Product Relevance

Market Pulse AI should use quality concepts when evaluating opportunity candidates.

An opportunity with weak liquidity, poor cash flow, heavy debt, or repeated dilution should be penalized even if price momentum is strong.

## Limitations

Quality can become expensive. A good business can still be a poor investment if valuation, expectations, or market conditions are unfavorable.

Quality analysis must be paired with valuation, technical context, catalysts, and risk.
