## Product Relevance

Market Pulse AI uses these concepts to shape product thinking around:

- Opportunity discovery
- Evidence quality
- Risk penalties
- Confidence scoring
- Portfolio awareness
- Market psychology
- Cross-market intelligence
- Explainable recommendations

The library should evolve as the product matures, but future updates should preserve the product's core values: evidence, explainability, transparency, and user trust.
