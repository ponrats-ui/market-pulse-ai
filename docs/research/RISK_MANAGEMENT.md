# Risk Management Research

## Purpose

Risk management studies how investors identify, measure, communicate, and respond to uncertainty.

Market Pulse AI treats risk as part of every opportunity, not a separate afterthought.

## Core Risk Types

- Market risk
- Liquidity risk
- Volatility risk
- Concentration risk
- Event risk
- Currency risk
- Interest-rate risk
- Fundamental deterioration
- Provider data risk
- Behavioral risk

## Product Relevance

The AI Opportunity Discovery Engine should reduce opportunity quality when severe risks are present.

Risk warnings should be clear, visible, and evidence-based.

## Limitations

Risk cannot be fully measured in advance. Some risks appear suddenly.

Market Pulse AI should encourage preparation, not imply complete control.
