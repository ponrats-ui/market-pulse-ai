# Small Cap Research

## Purpose

Small-cap research studies smaller listed companies that may offer growth potential but often carry higher risk.

Small caps can be inefficiently followed, which may create opportunity, but they can also be illiquid, volatile, and financially fragile.

## Common Research Areas

- Revenue growth
- Margin improvement
- Balance sheet strength
- Cash flow quality
- Management execution
- Ownership structure
- Liquidity
- Market opportunity
- Competitive position
- Catalysts

## Product Relevance

Market Pulse AI should treat small-cap opportunities with disciplined evidence standards.

The product should highlight both upside factors and survival risks.

## Limitations

Small-cap data can be sparse. News coverage may be limited. Spreads may be wide.

Missing data should reduce confidence.
